# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for ml-toolmatch

## Problem Statement

As a Series A/B startup with an ML team, finding the right tools and services can be a daunting task. The market is flooded with various options, each claiming to solve specific problems. This leads to:

*   Wasted time and resources on trial-and-error
*   Inefficient toolchains that hinder team productivity
*   Difficulty in scaling ML teams due to tooling complexity

## Target Users

*   ML Engineers and Researchers at Series A/B startups
*   ML Team Leads and Managers responsible for tooling decisions
*   Startup Founders and CTOs who need to optimize their ML teams' efficiency

## Goals

*   Help ML teams identify the most suitable tools and services for their specific needs
*   Streamline the tooling process, reducing time-to-market and increasing team productivity
*   Provide a trusted advisor service for ML teams to make informed tooling decisions

## Key Features (Prioritized)

### 1. Tool Profiling and Categorization

*   Develop a comprehensive database of ML tools and services
*   Categorize tools based on their functionality, industry, and use cases
*   Allow users to filter and search tools based on their specific needs

### 2. ML Team Profiling and Matching

*   Create a user-friendly interface for ML teams to describe their tooling needs and pain points
*   Develop a matching algorithm that recommends tools and services based on the team's profile and requirements
*   Provide a dashboard for teams to track their tooling progress and make adjustments as needed

### 3. Community Feedback and Reviews

*   Allow users to leave reviews and ratings for tools and services
*   Implement a community feedback system to help teams make informed decisions
*   Display aggregated feedback and ratings on tool profiles

### 4. Integration with Popular ML Frameworks and Libraries

*   Integrate ml-toolmatch with popular ML frameworks and libraries (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch)
*   Allow users to discover and integrate tools and services directly within their ML workflows

### 5. Premium Features and Support

*   Offer premium features, such as personalized tooling consultations and priority support
*   Provide a subscription-based model for access to premium features and exclusive content

## Success Metrics

*   User acquisition and retention rates
*   Time-to-market for ML teams using ml-toolmatch
*   User satisfaction ratings and Net Promoter Score (NPS)
*   Revenue growth and subscription conversion rates

## Scope

*   Develop a web-based platform for ml-toolmatch
*   Integrate with popular ML frameworks and libraries
*   Implement a comprehensive database of ML tools and services
*   Develop a user-friendly interface for ML teams to describe their tooling needs and pain points

## Out-of-Scope

*   Developing custom ML tools and services
*   Providing direct technical support for ML teams
*   Offering consulting services for ML team strategy and optimization
*   Integrating with non-ML related tools and services
