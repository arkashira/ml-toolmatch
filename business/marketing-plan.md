# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Primary: ml-toolmatch is the premier machine learning tooling marketplace for Series A/B startups to discover, evaluate, and acquire the best tools and services for their ML teams.
Alternatives:
1. ml-toolmatch simplifies machine learning tool procurement, empowering startups to focus on innovation rather than infrastructure.
2. ml-toolmatch is the trusted advisor for ML teams, providing personalized tool recommendations and streamlining the procurement process.
3. ml-toolmatch unlocks machine learning potential by connecting startups with the right tools, services, and expertise.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Alex Chen**, ML Engineer at a Series A startup, responsible for building and deploying ML models, with a daily job of evaluating and implementing new tools, and a budget of $10,000/month for ML tooling.
2. **Maya Patel**, Director of Engineering at a Series B startup, overseeing ML team operations, with a daily job of ensuring ML team productivity and efficiency, and a budget of $50,000/month for ML tooling and services.
3. **Ethan Kim**, Founder and CEO of a Series A startup, responsible for making strategic decisions about ML investments, with a daily job of balancing business growth and technical capabilities, and a budget of $20,000/month for ML tooling and consulting services.

## Channels
Top 3 channels with estimated Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):
1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Targeted ads to reach ML engineers, directors of engineering, and founders of Series A/B startups, with an estimated CAC of $500.
2. **ML-focused Conferences and Meetups**: Sponsorship and speaking opportunities to connect with potential customers, with an estimated CAC of $1,000.
3. **Content Marketing (Blog and Newsletter)**: Creation of high-quality content to attract and engage with the target audience, with an estimated CAC of $200.

## Content Cadence
Week-by-week content plan:
Week 1-2: Introduction to ml-toolmatch and its benefits
Week 3-4: Case studies of successful ML tool implementations
Week 5-6: Webinar on "Best Practices for ML Tool Selection"
Week 7-8: Interview with an ML expert on "The Future of ML Tooling"
Week 9-10: Launch of a free ML tooling guide
Week 11-12: Announcement of a limited-time discount for early adopters

## Launch Milestones
* D-30: Finalize marketing plan and launch preparation
* D-0: Launch ml-toolmatch and announce to the public
* D+30: Analyze early adoption metrics and gather customer feedback
* D+90: Evaluate the success of the go-to-market plan and plan for future growth

## Success Metrics
* Daily Active Users (DAU): 100 by D+30, 500 by D+90
* Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR): $10,000 by D+30, $50,000 by D+90
